LISTEN: Apocalyptica and Wagner

Widely held as one of the most exiting groups in the rock world today, the ground-breaking, edge-cutting, hair-raising Finnish orchestral band Apocalyptica are getting acquainted with Wagner.

The Hot Press Newsdesk, 13 Nov 2013, 17:26



Wagner Reloaded- Live in Leipiz looks like another phenomenal marriage of orchestra and metal from the classically trained quartet. The band, which consists of three cellists and a drummer, has gained worldwide respect and renown after seven hugely popular studio albums and sell out shows at both rock venues and concert halls across the globe.

Apocalypticas’ latest surprise, a series of compositions based on Richard Wagner’s work and life, promises to be no less unique then their previous projects. In Leipzig the band worked alongside award-winning choreographer and dancer Gregor Seyffert to create cross-genre celebration of live music and theatrical production, and the resulting live recording is both raw and dynamic.

The new album is available from November 19 on BMG. Watch the full stunning show here , or stream the album in its entirety below.
