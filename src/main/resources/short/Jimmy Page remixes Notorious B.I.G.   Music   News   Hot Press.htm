Jimmy Page remixes Notorious B.I.G.

Who knew? Led Zeppelin meets Hip Hop.

The Hot Press Newsdesk, 18 Nov 2013, 11:42



Jimmy Page, apparently, does remixes.

According to sources, including Page’s official website, the Led Zeppelin frontman and all round rock ‘n’ roll legend has united Notorious B.I.G.’s ‘Hypnotize’ with Zeppelin’s ‘Ramble On.’

The new track, which he calls ‘Ramblize,’ remains true to the late rap god’s Grammy-nominated single while sampling the original baseline, percussion and acoustic parts of Zeppelin’s famous track.

Random? Not entirely. UK’s Channel 4 has just uncovered some lost footage of Biggie, after which Page’s site skinned in a picture of the fallen rapper as a mark of respect.

Hear Robert Plant’s vocals mingle those of Biggie on Page’s website or his Soundcloud.
