Zac Brown and Dave Grohl join forces

An exciting collaboration has grown out of two brilliant artists bumping into each other at a clothing store.

The Hot Press Newsdesk, 02 Dec 2013, 16:42



Zac Brown, of country-rockers the Zac Brown Band, befriended Foo Fighters frontman Dave Grohl while picking up some altered clothes for the Grammy Awards. The two stayed in touch- and soon the award winning country band were rocking out with one of the industry’s most energetic and multi-talented artists.

The Grohl Sessions Vol. 1 comes out via iTunes on 10 December from Brown’s Southern Ground Artists label, and it promises to be fun listening.

“It’s still us” says Brown on album’s promo video, “but pushed to another limit.”

Grohl, who is extremely pleased with his latest collaboration, chuckled “everyone’s so good that you challenge each other to be more and more badass!”

So badass, in fact, that he decided to leave much of the fiddly editing process out, explaining to Rolling Stone “we didn't fuck with computers, we tracked live, four-part harmonies around one microphone. It’s rocking.”

The Grohl Sessions Vol. 2 , a making-of documentary and physical releases will follow up next year. Get a taster below.
