Stream the Soundtrack: The Hunger Games

Ahead of its official release date, The Hunger Games: Catching Fire soundtrack is now streaming in full

The Hot Press Newsdesk, 14 Nov 2013, 14:40



The soundtrack for The Hunger Games: Catching Fire is lavishly star-studded. And right now, it’s free to stream on itunes. Artists such as The National, Coldplay, the Weekend, Santigold, Antony & The Johnsons, Patti Smith, Lorde and Christina Aguilera- to name a few- have all contributed to the sound of the series’ second movie.

Coldplay’s new track, ‘Atlas,’ is the first piece that the band has ever written for a movie, and their first song released since 2011. The movie’s director, Francis Lawrence, comments;

“I have great respect and admiration for Coldplay, and we are thrilled with how well they have connected to the themes and ideas within the film. Their unwavering passion and excitement for the project elevated the collaboration even further.”

Not simply the latest sensation among young adult readers, The Hunger Games series is a mature and complex dystopian tale which draws on past and present political regimes to deliver a powerful and potent reflection of our world.

The Hunger Games: Catching Fire lands in cinemas on November 22nd.
