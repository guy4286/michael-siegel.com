Stream Wanda Jackson’s White Stripes Cover

Rockabilly Queen and all round music legend Wanda Jackson has recorded an exciting new cover version of The White Stripes' ‘In The Cold, Cold Night’ for a very special rock tribute album.

The Hot Press Newsdesk, 13 Nov 2013, 12:24



At only 38, Jack White has commanded respect right across the music industry, influencing a staggeringly diverse array of styles. Now masters of rockabilly, early rock, country and classic punk have created a unique album in tribute to White’s inspiring career.

Not your average straightforward covers record, it features legends such as Big Jay McNeely, Sonny Burgess, Gary U.S. Bonds, Bobby Vee, Walter Lure of Johnny Thunders' The Heartbreakers, Joe Clay and Rosie Flores performing their own inventive and original takes on White’s work.

The homage to the modern bluesman, entitled Rockin' Legends Pay Tribute to Jack White, is due to be released on November 19 via Cleopatra Records. However, you can hear its first track, Wanda Jackson’s cover of ‘In the Cold, Cold Night,’ via Rolling Stone . right now.
