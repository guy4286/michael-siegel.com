Watch: Tom Odell’s new video

The song, titled ‘I Know,’ comes from Odell’s chart-topping debut album Long Way Down

The Hot Press Newsdesk, 02 Dec 2013, 13:59



The pop pianist, who was named 'Critics’ Choice' at the Brit Awards earlier this year and received 'Radio Forth Best Performance Award,' is certainly one to watch.

The video was shot in the beautiful Mörkö ( 'dark island') just south of Stockholm. Its director, Magnus Harder, said "we wanted to shoot a stripped down performance piece on Tom in some moody Scandinavian light. It’s something very subdued and at the same time, a very powerful song, so putting Tom in this alluring environment makes sense (at least for me)."

Odell gave a moving performance at Dublin’s Olympia on Saturday 2nd November, and is due to perform alongside the likes of Katy Perry and Sir Elton John as a special guest on the X Factor final later this month.
