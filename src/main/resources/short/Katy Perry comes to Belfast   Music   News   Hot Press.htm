Katy Perry comes to Belfast

Katy Perry’s 'Prismatic World Tour' of 2014 will kick off in Belfast’s Odyssey Arena on Wednesday 7 May, it has just been announced.

The Hot Press Newsdesk, 18 Nov 2013, 12:48



The global superstar tweeted the good news this morning, adding “DON'T WORRY - more GLOBAL tour dates coming soon! I can't wait to get closer to ALL of you than ever before! ‪#THEPRISMATICWORLDTOUR." After Belfast, the show goes to Newcastle, Nottingham, Birmingham, Glasgow, Manchester, Liverpool and Sheffield before playing three dates at London's O2 Arena.

The tour follows Katy’s chart topping new album Prism, which features the new track 'Unconditionally’ as well as the staggeringly popular lead single 'Roar.'

‘Roar,’ which has already sold over five million singles worldwide, has gone Platinum not only in the UK but in Italy, Mexico, Norway, Ireland and Sweden. It has certified Gold in 10 countries, become the most sold single in chart history in Canada, and is this first song this year to enjoy a whole 9 weeks as the No.1 airplay record in the UK.

For the UK leg of the world tour the star will perform alongside special guests Icona Pop, the synth-pop duo from Sweden whose addictive song ‘I Love It’ quickly became one of the biggest hits of 2013. The show promises to be glamorous, glossy and magnificent. It even includes a special standing pit in front of the stage called ‘The Reflection Section’ where Katy will get up close and personal with her adoring fans.

Tickets go on-sale 9am on Friday 22 November from Ticketmaster .
